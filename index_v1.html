Belgium

country of northwestern Europe. It is one of the smallest and most densely populated European countries, and it has been, since its independence in 1830, a representative democracy headed by a hereditary constitutional monarch. Initially, Belgium had a unitary form of government.

Belgium is not only the land of beer,waffels chocolate, and some of the best music festivals in the world but also of diamonds, amazing folk festivals, and a record-breaking political gridlock.
